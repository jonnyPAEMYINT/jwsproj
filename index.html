What we do

JP SALES & MKT CO. LTD is a Hong Kong-registered trading enterprise with over 20 years of experience in global sourcing and distribution. Led by Managing Director Mr. Jonathan W. Smith, a British citizen, our company operates primarily from Thailand.

As a trusted sourcing agent, we focus on delivering high-quality products, developing new markets for our suppliers, and building long-term partnerships worldwide.

Focus

We specialize in three distinct trading sectors to serve a diverse range of market needs.

  • 01 Seafood and Marine Products
  • 02 Soft Commodities
  • 03 Hard Commodities

01 Seafood and Marine Products

Our extensive seafood portfolio includes:

  • frozen crabs
  • lobsters
  • freshwater and saltwater fish
  • octopus
  • squid
  • shrimp
  • salmon and salmon by-products
  • value-added items tailored for retail, airline, and hospitality sectors

We provide chilled shrimp via air freight.

02 Soft Commodities

We trade cultivated natural products such as:

  • sugar
  • rice
  • wheat
  • poultry
  • meats

Providing essential commodities for food, retail, and food service industries.

03 Hard Commodities

In addition to food products, we deal with:

  • petrochemical products
  • refined fuels
  • bamboo pulp

and various raw materials supporting industries requiring sustainable and processed commodities.

Operations

Our global operations span markets including Southeast Asia, China, Australia, Hong Kong, Japan, the Middle East, Europe, and North America.

Markets

Primarily operating in spot markets, we emphasize reliability, quality, and market responsiveness to meet evolving industry demands.
